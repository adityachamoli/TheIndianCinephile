About Our Platform

The Indian Cinephile is a social media platform for sharing love and passion for cinema. It provides all the information and details regarding critically acclaimed and fascinating movies and television shows at a single place. Epic monlogues and cinematographic shots with deep pop-cultural impact are shared. You can provide reviews and ratings for movies and television shows as it will help others to sort out their weekend watchlists.
